Our Story — From Fire to Finance

Named after a man who refused to fear.

🔥
“Luuuunooo, Luuuunooo… Luno amechoma jando.”
“If these spirits are so mighty, why can they not stop the hunger, disease, and oppression we face every day?”

In 1970s Southern Tanzania, tradition was law and rituals were never questioned. Luno was a young man who had never set foot in a classroom — yet he carried a logic stronger than blind fear.

To test the spirits, he placed a hot coal in the corner of a sacred jando hut and watched it burn to the ground. When the curses everyone expected never came, Luno asked his community that one powerful question.

We built LUNO to carry that same spirit of defiance into the modern transport sector. For too long the bodaboda economy has been held back by old habits — messy notebooks, fading receipts, endless disputes. LUNO replaces the unreliable old way with a permanent, automated, human-first system. It is time to stop doing things the old way.

The Reality on Our Roads

Hard work, no record to show for it.

Across East Africa, a million riders move the economy on two wheels — yet they remain unprotected, unbanked, and unseen. LUNO was built to change that.

Notebook chaos

Payments tracked in tattered notebooks and memory. Lost receipts and disputed totals strain the trust between owner and driver.

No financial identity

Without records, hard-working drivers can't access loans, insurance, or recognition — trapped outside the formal economy.

No safety net

When a driver is attacked on the road, help is far away and families learn too late. Dignity and safety should not be a luxury.

How It Works

From first ride to full ownership.

Four simple steps turn a rent-to-own arrangement into a clear, dignified journey — for both driver and owner.

1

Set the contract

The owner registers the bike, the driver, and a weekly payment. LUNO calculates the path to ownership.

2

Pay & verify

The driver pays via M-Pesa or cash. Each payment is recorded and verified — permanent digital proof.

3

Watch it grow

The ownership ring fills in real time. The driver sees their exact freedom date draw closer every week.

4

Own it

The final payment lands. The bike is theirs — and a verified financial identity is born for life.

The Founder

A doctor. A fleet owner. A builder.

LUNO was created by an Emergency Medicine doctor at Tanzania's national hospital — who is also a bodaboda & bajaji fleet owner. He treats the riders a broken system breaks, and runs the very business LUNO digitizes. He built the entire platform himself, to serve the people upstream — before they arrive broken.
